About Me

Greetings and salutations! I’m Mollie, a self-proclaimed jane of all trades and web developer based in Charlotte, NC. In 2017, I made the decision to break into the tech industry with no experience whatsoever (unless you count customizing my MySpace page) after seeing an ad for UNC Charlotte’s Full Stack Coding Bootcamp.
